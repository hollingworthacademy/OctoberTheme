Each year we also have two native speaker Foreign Language Assistants working with the department from October to May.

The MFL rooms are each fitted with state of the art facilities such as Interactive Whiteboards and Visualisers. Each room also has access to laptops and computers and the facilities are accessible to pupils both within lessons and after school to assist with independent learning and homework.

The Curriculum

Key Stage 3

Pupils in Years 7, 8 & 9 receive two 60 minute lessons a week with homework set once a fortnight, as is the policy at Hollingworth. Progress is tracked and monitored in accordance with agreed procedures. Pupils complete a baseline assessment the first half term in Year 7 and receive two assessments a year. Pupils have the opportunity to study a second language, Italian as part of our Year 9 Preference Curriculum.


GCSE (Key Stage 4)

Pupils in GCSE classes receive three 60 minute lessons a week with homework one to three times a week. The two year GCSE course is awarded with the Edexcel examination board which is 100% exam (divided into a speaking exam and listening, reading and writing papers each worth 25%). Progress is tracked and monitored in accordance with agreed procedure. Pupils complete a range of both formal and informal assessments in the form of extended writing and speaking activities in class, as well as mock and real examinations.
