We are looking to increase our bank of Examination Invigilators, to help supervise and ensure efficient running of the School’s examinations. Invigilators will be required to work during examination periods, which run at various times throughout the year, with busier periods during November, December, March, May and June. Successful candidates will be employed on a casual, ad-hoc basis, with particularly heavy periods being in May and June each year.

Exam Invigilators are required to work with school staff, candidates and other invigilators before, during and after examinations to ensure the smooth running of examinations in accordance with published guidelines.

The role will require individuals to work with candidates as part of a team or as a lone invigilator. Full training will be given. Successful candidates will have excellent communication skills; a confident and assertive manner and a flexible approach to working hours.

  • £9.00 per hour
  • As and when required during examination periods, particularly busy periods in May and June
  • Casual Hours (Regular hours are not guaranteed)
  • Start Date: ASAP
  • Closing Date: Noon on Thursday 31st January 2019
  • Interviews : To take place week commencing 4th February 2019

The College is committed to safeguarding and promoting the welfare of children and young people and expects all staff and volunteers to share this commitment. Appointment to these posts is subject to an enhanced CRB check.
