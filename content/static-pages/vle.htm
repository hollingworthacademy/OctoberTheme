How to Access the VLE as a Student

  1. Go to the VLE login page: https://hollingworth.fireflycloud.net
  2. Click on the Office 365 link.
  3. If you are accessing the VLE at school you will be taken straight to your VLE dashboard.
  4. If you are accessing the VLE at home you will be taken to the school's Office 365 login page where you will need to enter your username and password. Your username is your normal school username followed by @yrXX.hollingworthacademy.co.uk where XX is the year you started in Year 7, for example, if your school username is jsmith1 and you started Year 7 in 2017 then your Office 365 username will be jsmith1@yr17.hollingworthacademy.co.uk. Your password is your normal school password.
