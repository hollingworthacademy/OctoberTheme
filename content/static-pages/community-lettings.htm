Hollingworth has a number of indoor and outdoor facilities available for hire to the community including a spacious indoor sports hall and a fantastic 3G all-weather, floodlit, artificial pitch suitable for a wide range of activities.

We invite you to come and look round the facilities here at Hollingworth, please telephone 01706 292824 to arrange your viewing tour.

To make a booking please download, complete and return the lettings form.
