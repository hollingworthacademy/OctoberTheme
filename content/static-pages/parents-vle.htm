Registering to Access the VLE

  1. Go to the VLE login page: https://hollingworth.fireflycloud.net
  2. Activate your account by clicking the link in the green panel and entering the email that we have on file for you.
  3. You will receive and email with an activation link, please check your junk folder if you don't before reporting an issue.
  4. Click the link in the email and create a password for your account.
  5. You should now see your dashboard and have access to your child/children's VLE areas.
