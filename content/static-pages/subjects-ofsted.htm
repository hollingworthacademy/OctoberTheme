n 2008 Hollingworth Business & Enterprise College received an "Outstanding" report from Ofsted. Ofsted praised the College for its pupils' achievements and attributed that to its outstanding teaching and learning. The College also underwent a number of inspections covering Modern Foreign Languages, Literacy and Curriculum Innovation.

In 2013 Hollingworth Business & Enterprise College became Hollingworth Academy after converting to Academy status.
